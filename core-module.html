Core Module

  • Multi-currency Accounts and IBANs

    Provides the ability to create multi-currency accounts for users, allowing them to conduct business in various currencies with minimal conversion costs. Each account is accompanied by an individual IBAN, facilitating international payments and transfers.

  • Flexible End-user Role Management

    This tool allows for the configuration of various levels of access and permissions for different users in the system, ensuring security and effective information management.

  • Business Accounts

    Especially useful for corporate clients, this feature allows the creation of multi-currency business accounts in IBAN format. This ensures flexibility and convenience for companies conducting business in different countries.

  • Real-Time Profit/Loss Monitoring

    Provides business owners and management with the ability to track their financial results in real-time.

  • Fee Plans and Price Lists

    Manages fee plans and pricing, offering flexibility and customization to meet various business models and customer needs. Individual configurations are available for each client.

  • Online Management Reporting

    This tool enables the generation of various types of management reports online, facilitating the analysis of business processes and aiding in making strategic decisions.

  • Multi-language Support

    Support for multiple languages facilitates the use of the product by clients from different countries, making it more accessible and understandable for an international audience.
